About me

Hi! this is Renato Hermoza. I'm a second year PhD student at the University of Adelaide, my work focuses on applying machine learning to medical images and currently working on weakly supervised localization.

Things I'm interested in no particular order: machine learning: data visualization, optimization, simulation, open source...

I'm always up to apply my knowledge on impactful and social projects. If you are interested in doing a collaboration, send me a mail: renato.hermoza@pucp.edu.pe.
